# MCN Privacy Boundary

## Internal Only

Do not include these fields in brand-safe exports:

- personal phone, WeChat, private email, ID information
- bottom price, split ratio, rebate, payment dispute detail
- negative personality notes
- hard-to-communicate notes
- breach, delay, non-delivery, or payment risk details
- contract scans, signatures, certificates, official seals
- raw chat logs unless the user explicitly confirms the export scope

## Brand Safe

Brand-safe creator output may include:

- public platform handles
- public content direction
- audience and performance range
- suitable categories
- public brand cases with user confirmation
- standard deliverables
- non-sensitive availability notes

Brand-safe brand output may include:

- brand name
- category
- campaign objective
- content requirements
- platform and content format
- public collaboration status

## Rule

When unsure, keep the field internal and mark it `privacy: internal`.
