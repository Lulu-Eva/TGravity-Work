# Collaboration Fields

## Required Working Fields

- brand_id or brand_name
- creator_id or creator_name
- brief_id if available
- project_name
- platforms
- deliverables
- quoted_price
- final_price
- status
- payment_status
- result_summary
- review_summary
- source_evidence

## Recommended Questions

Ask at most 3.

Priority order:

1. Which brand and creator does this cooperation bind to?
2. What stage is it in: quote, contract, execution, acceptance, payment, closed, paused, or terminated?
3. What result or next action should be recorded?
