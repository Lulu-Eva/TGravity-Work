# Brand Profile Fields

## Public / Working Fields

- name
- aliases
- category
- contact_source
- relationship_strength
- budget_hint
- preferred_scenarios
- current_stage
- next_action

## Internal

- private contact
- decision chain notes
- payment_cycle
- review_rounds
- communication_cost
- change risk
- budget weakness
- legal or compliance risk

## Recommended Questions

Ask at most 3.

Priority order:

1. What category and product does this brand sell?
2. What is the current demand or stage?
3. What is the next action and who owns it?
