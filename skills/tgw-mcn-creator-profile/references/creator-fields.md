# Creator Profile Fields

## Public / Brand Safe

- name
- aliases
- platforms
- public handles
- content_direction
- audience_profile
- follower_snapshot
- performance_snapshot
- fit_categories
- public collaboration cases confirmed by the user

## Internal

- private contact
- bottom price
- split rules
- hard-to-communicate notes
- delivery_risks
- negative notes
- owner assessment
- payment or breach risk

## Recommended Questions

Ask at most 3.

Priority order:

1. Which platform/account is this creator on?
2. What content direction and audience are clear?
3. What is the next action: observe, contact, quote, match, pause, or archive?
