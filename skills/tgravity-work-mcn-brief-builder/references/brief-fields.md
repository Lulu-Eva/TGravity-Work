# Reverse Brief Fields

## Required Working Fields

- brand_id or brand_name
- product_name
- campaign_goal
- platforms
- budget_hint
- content_formats
- creator_requirements
- posting_date or schedule
- prohibitions
- review_process
- acceptance_method
- missing_info

## Creator Matching Fields

- recommended_creators
- rejected_creators
- fit_reason
- exclusion_reason

## Recommended Questions

Ask at most 3.

Priority order:

1. What result does the brand actually want?
2. What are the hard prohibitions?
3. What budget, deadline, and approval process are real?
